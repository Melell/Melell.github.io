About

Shadow Racer is a third-person driving adventure game set in a post-apocalyptic Grand Canyon. The player must find and use the ancient powers of the flash, turbo and bumper in order to defeat the cars from the darkness empire. It was published on Steam in May 2022 to a majority of positive reviews.
